About Ledger Studio

We think invoicing deserves better than a spreadsheet.

Ledger Studio started as a simple question: why does the last step of getting paid — the invoice — so often look like an afterthought? We set out to make it the opposite.

Our belief

Paperwork is part of the craft, not separate from it.

The invoice is often the last thing a client sees from you before they pay. We believe it should look as considered as the work it's billing for — clear, calm, and unmistakably yours.

So we built Ledger Studio around three ideas: your brand should carry through automatically, your numbers should be trustworthy at a glance, and the whole process should take minutes, not an afternoon.

STEP ONE

Set up once

Your logo, company details and bank information, saved for every invoice that follows.

STEP TWO

Bill with confidence

Clean line items, automatic totals, and a preview that matches exactly what your client receives.

STEP THREE

Track what's coming in

A dashboard that shows what you've earned and what's still outstanding, filtered your way.

What we stand for

The values behind every screen.

01

Clarity over clutter

Every field earns its place. If it doesn't help you get paid faster, it's not on the page.

02

Your data stays yours

Your company details, bank information and invoices are stored on your own device, not on a server you don't control.

03

Built for real work

Designed around how freelancers and small studios actually bill clients — not how a big accounting suite thinks you should.

04

Quietly beautiful

A tool you open every week should be pleasant to look at. We spent as much time on the motion and detail as the math.
